Battle of the Blue Bloods:

The recent history of Indiana and North Carolina men's basketball

Photo illustration by Emma Pawlitz

The Hoosiers' most highly anticipated game of the year has finally arrived.

No. 10 Indiana men's basketball welcomes No. 18 University of North Carolina to Simon Skjodt Assembly Hall on Wednesday night for the ultimate iteration of the ACC-Big Ten Challenge. The matchup might look different from what fans expected when the Tar Heels were the top team in the country less than a week ago, but there is still plenty to unpack ahead of tipoff.

The two storied programs have a surprisingly deep recent history, including many meaningful relationships and familial connections. So sit back, relax and prepare for the 9:15 p.m. tipoff by reading the tales of the lifelong best friends, the hometown hero and the dominant down-low duo.
