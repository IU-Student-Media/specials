IU Homecoming Parade welcomes students, community groups to celebrate Hoosier spirit

A member of the 2022 Homecoming court tosses candy to kids Oct. 7, 2022, on Woodlawn Avenue. The 12 members of the court represented various student groups, such as Kelley Student Government and the IU Student Foundation.

The 2022 IU Homecoming Parade starts moving along the route at 5:30 p.m. Oct. 7, 2022, at the intersection of East 17th Street and Woodlawn Avenue. The parade kicked off Homecoming weekend in Bloomington.

A member of the Student Athletic Board hypes up Hoosier fans during the 2022 Homecoming Parade on Oct. 7, 2022, on Woodlawn Avenue. Both student and community groups were able to walk in the parade.

A group with Dave O'Guinn, vice provost for student affairs and dean of students, cheers with Indiana fans during the IU Homecoming Parade on Oct. 7, 2022, on Woodlawn Avenue. The parade went south to 13th Street before heading north on Fee Lane.

Representatives of the Hoosier Scholars program wave Indiana flags Oct. 7, 2022, in the IU Homecoming Parade on Fee Lane. Both student and community groups were able to walk in the parade.

Shaylee Barnes, Miss Indiana University 2022, waves at Hoosier fans during the IU Homecoming Parade on Oct. 7, 2022, on Woodlawn Avenue. The parade kicked off Homecoming weekend.

Andy Morris plays piano on a float for the Kelley School of Business in the IU Homecoming Parade on Oct. 7, 2022, on Woodlawn Avenue. Morris' musical skills have earned him over 200,000 followers on Instagram.

A representative of the IU Student Foundation rides in the IU Homecoming Parade on Oct. 7, 2022, on Woodlawn Avenue. The foundation puts on the annual Little 500 race.

A representative of the School of Public Health Living Learning Community smiles at Hoosier fans during the IU Homecoming Parade on Oct. 7, 2022, on 17th Street. Both student and community groups were able to walk in the parade.

A member of the Marching Hundred plays in the IU Homecoming Parade on Oct. 7, 2022, on 13th Street. The band closed out the parade.

The Marching Hundred plays in the IU Homecoming Parade on Oct. 7, 2022, on 13th Street. The band closed out the parade.
