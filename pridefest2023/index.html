Scenes from Bloomington Pridefest 2023








Photos by : Audrey Engel
Aug. 26, 2023
01
PrideFest kicks off Aug. 26, 2023, on Kirkwood Avenue. Banners and flags marked the start of the annual festival.
Artist Dom Darko is seen at PrideFest on Aug. 26, 2023, on Kirkwood Avenue. His booth showcased his artwork including paintings, earrings and pins.
02
painting hangs in Dom Darko’s PrideFest booth on Aug. 26, 2023, on Kirkwood Avenue. Darko’s walls were lined with his art and various pieces of pride gear.
03
Mullins (left) and Oriana Barleston (right) are photographed at Barleston’s PrideFest booth on Aug. 26, 2023, on Kirkwood Avenue. Barleston’s booth showcased their crocheted pieces, offering items such as hats and mug covers.
04
Tyler Dieden is photographed at PrideFest on Aug. 26, 2023, on Kirkwood Avenue. Dieden’s rainbow eye makeup matched the flags and banners displayed in booths.
05
The IU LGBTQ+ Project booth is seen at PrideFest, Aug. 26, 2023, on Kirkwood Avenue. People of all ages attended PrideFest, enjoying beverages and snacks.
06
Adelaide Young Brust poses for a photo on Aug. 26, 2023, on Kirkwood Avenue. Wearing a predominantly black outfit, Young Brust contrasted it with a rainbow fan.
