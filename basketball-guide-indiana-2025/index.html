Indiana basketball guide 2025-26

Indiana men’s and women’s basketball both finished their 2025-26 preseason games undefeated. The men pulled off victories over Marion University on Oct. 17 and Baylor University on Oct. 26. The women defeated Missouri University of Science and Technology 100-32 on Oct. 27. The Hoosiers are set to kick off Big Ten play in December, with the men taking on Minnesota Dec. 3 and the women facing Illinois on Dec. 6.
