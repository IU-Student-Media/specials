June First Friday Gallery Walk
Lynne Gilliatt (left) and Jean Haley (right) pose for a photo at First Friday Gallery Walk, June 2, 2023, at the Vault at Gallery Mortgage. The two artists converged their art into a collaborative exhibit of fiber art installations called "Wool Wanderings."
01
Haley Gilliatt's work is photographed at the First Friday gallery opening, June 2, 2023, at the Vault at Gallery Mortgage. The mission of the gallery is to give back to the community by highlighting local artists and supporting local charitable organizations.
02
"Wool Wandering 12" by Lynne Gilliatt is displayed June 2, 2023, at First Fridays Gallery Walk. The piece is a part of a larger collaborative exhibit of fiber art installations called "Wool Wanderings" at the Vault at Gallery Mortgage.
03
"Wool Wandering 14" by Lynne Gilliatt is photographed, June 2, 2023, at First Friday Gallery Walk. Gilliatt, a long-established artist, used her painterly technique in a new way on wool, for the first time, to create the collaborative exhibit.
04
Caleb Poer paints a temporary mural June 2, 2023, at First Friday Gallery Walk. First Friday includes 14 local Bloomington gallery exhibits and entertainment from 5-8 p.m. in the evening.
05
The Bloomington crowd is seen at First Friday Gallery walk, June 2, 2023, in front of Pili's Party Taco Truck. First Friday is a monthly event to showcase the local flavor of Bloomington along the Arts Mile and beyond.
06
The "Game Show Superpower Apocalypse" series by David Gantz is seen June 2, 2023, at the I Fell Gallery. The mixed media pieces in the exhibit were inspired by comics, game shows and the absurdity of media.
07
The "Game Show Superpower Apocalypse" series by David Gantz is featured, June 2, 2023, at the I Fell Gallery. The Gallery also showcased the works of Brycen along and Luke Starks at its First Friday Gallery Walk.
