IU vs. Purdue

Help the IDS beat
the Exponent

TOP PHOTO Indiana football players hold up the Oaken Bucket on Nov. 30, 2019, in Ross-Ade Stadium in West Lafayette, Indiana. IDS file photo by Alex Deryn

The Indiana Daily Student is battling Purdue’s student newspaper, the Exponent, in its second annual rivalry challenge this November. Whichever publication raises more money before the Nov. 26 IU vs. Purdue football game “wins” the challenge, but all donations go to support student journalism at the respective publications. Keep an eye out for special content collaborations between the IDS and the Exponent!

Through a donation to the IDS Legacy Fund, you can help support the Hoosiers who keep you informed. Where does the money go? It helps pay more than 150 staff members, fund student travel to conferences and sports games and launch special projects, like our upcoming multiplatform election special.

We challenge you to help IU beat Purdue by supporting your award-winning independent student newspaper.
