Headline for transfer portal stuff

With the basketball season complete, the transfer portal is now fully up-and-running. Thousands of players on both the men's and women's side have entered the portal, eyeing a new location to spend the 2025-26 college basketball season. The portal closes April 22.

Here's your hub for all updates with both Indiana men's and women's basketball portal additions and departures.
