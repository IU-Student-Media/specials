Bloomington celebrates fourth annual Freezefest

A professional ice sculptor is pictured working at Bloomginton's fourth annual Freezefest Jan. 20, 2024 outside The Mill. Bloomington's only winter festival was met with low temperatures, sculptors were working in about 15°F Saturday. | IDS
