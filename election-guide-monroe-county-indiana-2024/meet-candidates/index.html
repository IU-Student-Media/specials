Meet the State and Federal Candidates

For the 2024 Primaries

Election season is upon us. Indiana’s Democratic and Republican primaries will occur May 7, selecting each party’s candidates for the November general election.

The IDS has localized our coverage to make sure you’re well-informed whenever and however you decide to vote. Along the top, you’ll find introductions to local and statewide candidates, along with our previous political coverage. Each section will be updated as we write full, detailed stories on candidates and information about each race.
