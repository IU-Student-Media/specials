Bands take main stage for 2024 Granfalloon finale

Lucia de la Garza, Eloise Wong and Bela Salazar of the Linda Lindas perform together on the main stage of the Granfalloon Festival, June 8, 2024, on Kirkwood Avenue in Bloomington, Indiana. During their performance, the Linda Lindas jumped off the stage, running to greet the front row of the audience.

Lucia de la Garza from the Linda Lindas performs on stage June 8, 2024, at the Granfalloon Festival on Kirkwood Avenue in Bloomington, Indiana. The Linda Lindas is an all-girl American band from Los Angeles.

The backup instrument players for Sleater-Kinney are seen on the main stage of the Granfalloon Festival, June 8, 2024, on Kirkwood Avenue in Bloomington, Indiana. Sleater-Kinney formed in 1994 in Olympia Washington during the Riot Grrrl movement.

The keyboard player for Sleater-Kinney appears amidst the smoke machines on the main stage of the Granfalloon Festival, June 8, 2024, on Kirkwood Avenue in Bloomington, Indiana. Sleater-Kinney originated as part of the Riot Grrrl movement and has become a key part of the feminist American indie rock scene.

Two girls are photographed bouncing during the performance of the Linda Lindas at the Granfalloon Festival, June 8, 2024, on Kirkwood Avenue in Bloomington, Indiana. The band performed several of their well known songs including the viral "Racist, Sexist Boy".

Corin Tucker of Sleater-Kinney performs during the last set of the night on the main stage of the Granfalloon Festival, June 8, 2024, on Kirkwood Avenue in Bloomington, Indiana. In the midst of the purple stage lights, Tucker performed alongside Carrie Brownstein on vocals and guitars.

Bela Salazar performs on stage June 8, 2024, at the Granfalloon Festival on Kirkwood Avenue in Bloomington, Indiana. The Linda Lindas went viral before the girls even graduated high school.

The audience looks on at the stage, June 8, 2024, during the Granfalloon Festival on Kirkwood Avenue in Bloomington, Indiana. As the second set of the night, the Linda Lindas brought excitement and energy to the crowds.
