How to Vote

IU students and Monroe County residents — take this quiz to learn your options

Nov. 8, Election Day, is coming up fast, and between absentee ballots, early voting and polling lines, making a voting plan can be hard.

With Indiana voter registration deadlines coming up Oct. 11, the Indiana Daily Student has put together a quiz to help you get your vote to the polls. Answer questions about registration, absentee eligibility and voting location to find guidelines, regulations and resources.
