Housing and Neighborhood Development (HAND)

Description

We are the city department tasked with inspecting and permitting all rental units within the Bloomington city limits. We maintain a file for every rental property, which is a matter of public record. Before you sign a lease, call our office and arrange to view the rental file for the property you are considering. You’ll be glad you did.
