Monroe County Fair shakes up the season with rides and ribbons

People work in a lemonade stand at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. The fair opened June 30 and runs until July 6.

Hollis Selo holds a buff laced Polish chicken during the small animal auction at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. Selo has been raising chickens like this one for three years as part of 4-H.

A woman holds a buff laced Polish chicken at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. Hollis Selo, a sophomore at Bloomington High School South, raised the chicken and sold it at Tuesday’s small animal auction.

The winning corn in the seed division of the Monroe County Fair sits in a display tent July 1, 2025. The gardening section of 4-H was judged June 26 and set up for attendees to survey during the rest of the fair.

4-H participants lead lambs around the showing area at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. 4-H is a national youth development organization often associated with raising livestock.

A 4-H participant holds a lamb during a judging session at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. 4-H'ers can raise and show animals such as chickens, rabbits, sheep and horses.

A man in an animatronic Tyrannosaurus Rex costume interacts with child spectators at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. He was a part of the “Dinosaur Show” exhibit which was at the fair from June 30 – July 5.

Jane Siedel smiles while shaking up lemons, sugar and water to make a lemon shake up at Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. She was helping her friends with their stall, which they had brought to the fair for the last decade.

A girl attempts to toss a ring over a bottle neck to win a prize at a stall at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. The carnival area of the fair had many different stalls where attendees could pay to play for a prize.

A Monroe County Fair attendee lifts her arms up on a swing ride at the fair July 1, 2025. The fair cost $5 for adults to attend, but tickets for the carnival had to be purchased separately.

A fair worker checks seatbelt safety prior to starting the swing ride at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. Most of the people who worked in the carnival section of the fair wore blue jumpsuits to easily identify them.

A Supercross racer stands up after crashing during a race in the Grandstand arena at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. The arena had different racing events throughout the week.

People watch Supercross races in the Grandstand arena at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. The arena will host a rodeo, go-kart races and a demolition derby during the fair.

Supercross racers drive over a hill in the arena at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. Supercross is a type of motocross racing that utilizes man-made dirt arenas.

A Supercross racer drives out of the starting line into the arena at the Monroe County Fair on July 1, 2025. Fair attendees could purchase a ticket to watch the arena races for $10.
